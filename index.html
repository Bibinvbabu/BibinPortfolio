My Projects

Leaf Disease Detection using Python & OpenCV

  • High-resolution images of leaves are captured using cameras or smartphones, ensuring clear and detailed visuals for analysis.
  • The captured images undergo preprocessing steps such as resizing, normalization, and noise reduction to enhance the quality and focus on the leaf area
  • Using computer vision techniques and machine learning models, the system analyzes the images to identify patterns and features indicative of specific leaf diseases.
  • Once a disease is detected, the affected area of the leaf is highlighted and detailed information about the disease, including symptoms, potential causes, and treatment recommendations, is generated and displayed.
  • The detected disease information and images are stored in a digital database, enabling easy retrieval, tracking of disease progression, and providing valuable data for further research and analysis.
  • Technologies used: Python3, OpenCV,Google Teachable Machine,Kaggle,firebase

Automated Hydroponics

  • Automated Hydroponics uses sensors to monitor nutrient levels, pH, temperature, and humidity in the hydroponic system
  • Sensors continuously collect data and transmit it to a cloud-based platform for real-time monitoring and analysis.
  • Based on the sensor readings, the system automatically adjusts nutrient delivery and pH levels to maintain optimal growing conditions.
  • This automation ensures precise nutrient delivery, enhancing plant growth and yield.
  • The integration of IoT and cloud technology enables efficient, data-driven management of hydroponic farms, reducing manual intervention and improving productivity.
  • Technologies used: C++, Esp32,IoT,Blynk Cloud.

Spot Welder

  • A spot welder is specifically used for welding nickel strips, which serve as connectors between individual battery cells in a battery pack..
  • The spot welder delivers a controlled, localized electrical current to the nickel strips, generating heat and fusing the strips to the battery terminals without damaging the cells.
  • Using a spot welder minimizes the risk of overheating or damaging the battery cells, which can occur with other welding methods, making it a safer choice for assembling battery packs.
  • Technologies used: Transformer,Arduino,C++.
